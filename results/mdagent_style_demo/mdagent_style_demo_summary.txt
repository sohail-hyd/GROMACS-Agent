MDAgent-style GROMACS-Agent demo

The implemented GROMACS-Agent was tested on five natural-language GROMACS workflow tasks. Each task was processed through ManagerAgent, PlannerAgent, Retriever, GromacsWorkerAgent, GromacsEvaluatorAgent, and CodeExecutorAgent. The final workflow, evaluation JSON, retrieved examples, and MDAgent-style transcript were saved for each run.

All accepted tasks demonstrate that the system is a runnable multi-agent research prototype, not only a manuscript concept.
