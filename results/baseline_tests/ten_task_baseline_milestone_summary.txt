Ten-task GSCF baseline milestone summary

Dataset status:
The first 10 GROMACS workflow-generation records were created and evaluated.

Completed GSCF tasks:
1. GSCF_001: pure water density at 300 K and 1 bar
2. GSCF_002: oxygen-oxygen RDF
3. GSCF_003: MSD/self-diffusion workflow
4. GSCF_004: hydrogen-bond number analysis
5. GSCF_005: z-direction density profile
6. GSCF_006: oxygen-hydrogen RDF
7. GSCF_007: oxygen-oxygen coordination number from RDF
8. GSCF_008: equilibration validation using temperature, pressure, density, energy, and volume
9. GSCF_009: trajectory preprocessing and PBC correction
10. GSCF_010: temperature time-series extraction and validation

Main finding:
The manual LLM baseline generally produced strong GROMACS workflows, with scores mostly between 8 and 9 out of 10.

Important recurring weaknesses:
1. Some workflows need safer topology-preparation wording.
2. Some GROMACS commands need clearer non-interactive selection handling.
3. Short-trajectory limitations must be stated more clearly.
4. Analysis-specific caveats are often incomplete, such as finite-size effects for diffusion or atom-name checking for RDF.
5. Final validation notes are sometimes present but not clearly separated.

Correction-loop result so far:
Evaluator-guided correction improved several weaker workflows, including MSD diffusion, density-profile analysis, and trajectory preprocessing.

Status:
This completes the first 10-task GSCF prototype milestone. The next stage is to expand GEQS evaluator records and build result tables/figures for the paper.
