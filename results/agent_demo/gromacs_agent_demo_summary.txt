GROMACS-Agent runnable prototype demonstration

Five natural-language GROMACS workflow-generation tasks were tested using the implemented GROMACS-Agent prototype.

Demo tasks:
1. O-O radial distribution function of pure water at 300 K
2. Temperature time-series extraction of pure water at 300 K
3. Density calculation of pure water at 300 K
4. MSD/diffusion workflow of pure water at 300 K
5. PBC trajectory preprocessing workflow for pure water

For each task, the agent performed:
- task interpretation,
- planning,
- retrieval from validated GSCF examples,
- workflow generation,
- evaluator scoring,
- correction-loop checking,
- saving of final workflow and evaluation files.

Result:
All five demo workflows were accepted by the evaluator with final score 10/10.

Interpretation:
This confirms that GROMACS-Agent is implemented as a runnable research prototype rather than only a conceptual framework.
